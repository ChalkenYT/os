Information

Opening windows

Open a window by selecting the button that represents that window. Simple enough!

Resizing windows

You can change the size of a window by dragging the grey box at the bottom right of the window. If it turns blue, you accidentally highlighted the window and just click off it and it will fix.

Closing windows

Hit the little "X" at the top right of the window to close it
